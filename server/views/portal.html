Planet Blitz

• Death Match - get the most kills!

• Each player controls 3 robots

• Scoreboard is at the top right corner

Your robots

• Red line above robot is the health

• Click on the robot to see how far the robot can move

• 1, 2, 3 keys provide fast switching between robots

Moving robots

• To move a robot, click on any green cell after selecting the robot


• The green line above the robot is the movement cooldown.

Shooting

• To shoot, right click in the direction you want to fire at


• The blue line above the robot indicates the shooting cooldown

Camera Navigation

• Pan around by moving your cursor to the edges of the screen

• Zoom in and out by scrolling down or up

• Rotate your view by pressing 'q' for clockwise and 'e' for counter-clockwise rotation
